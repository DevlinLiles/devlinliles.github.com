Devlin Liles is a Senior Consultant at Improving Enterprises. Since 2007 he has been presenting on software development practices and tools to audiences around the Texas, Arkansas and Oklahoma area. He is a regular presenter at the Tyson .NET user group and is a regional presenter at user groups and events. He founded the Tyson Development Conference as an internal Software Craftsmanship conference during his tenure there, as well as led the Tyson User group for a year and a half.
